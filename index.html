I am an assistant professor in the Department of East Asian Studies at the University of Toronto, specializing in the intellectual, literary, and cultural history of late imperial China and early modern East Asia. I am currently working on two larger projects: one on Manchu intellectual and literary culture, and one on early modern encyclopedism. This website contains information on my publications and latest research, as well as teaching.

Email: nathan.vedal [at] utoronto.ca
